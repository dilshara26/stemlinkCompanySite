Grow your home business with Social Videos

10 Feb 2022 3 Min read

Renting or purchasing a commercial workplace would be an expensive investment to anyone starting up a business. If we walk back to 10 years to 15 years, the company with the biggest showroom in the city will attract all the clients, and it was hard for a small company to climb up the ladder. But with this massive revolution in the digital market opened up a better opportunity for online sales.

Rise of Home Businesses

The most important thing with online businesses is that you don't need a physical workspace to sell your product. The digital marketing strategy and the quality of products and services you provide would mainly decide the growth of your business. Home businesses became a great source of income for individuals with creative ideas. All that takes to start a home business is an innovative idea on product or service, an initial investment to purchase essentials and a bedroom or a storeroom that could be converted for a workplace. Home businesses may include selling handmade products, dropshipping, purchasing bulk goods and selling pieces online, a subscription box business, teaching online or even creating a follow base that you could monetize other products.

Importance of Digital Marketing for Home Businesses

Since home businesses are not physically dealing with their customers, it's essential to have a better marketing strategy to reach out to clients and build brand loyalty. Social media and online platforms would carry a significant role here. An advertising executive, Fred R. Barnard, once said, "A picture is worth a thousand words." You have to multiply it by another thousand to relate the quote to short videos. Creative arts are an excellent source of communication to people, and using them mindfully towards gaining customers would be a better option in your marketing strategy. Surveys conducted on these fields have discovered that video marketing has increased brand awareness by 70%, traffic by 51%, and sales by 34%, which is a pretty interesting figure to consider.

When a business is planning on social video promotions, it's essential to figure out the audience they are interacting with and targeting, which would be beneficial to figure out the social media platforms they will use. TikTok, Youtube and Instagram surround a majority of users that could bring turnovers to the company.

Ideas to begin a social video series

A customer's brand loyalty starts from getting to know the brands they are dealing with the quality of its product or service. Creating an introductory video that explains who you are, what you do, what kind of solutions you can provide for the customers, and why your product or service becomes unique from competitors would build the first impression in customers. Having the video duration under 90 seconds would ensure that most users would watch it until the end. This video could be included on the home page or the page that gets the most acquisitions. Try creating a bit longer and more detailed videos as your customer base grows.

Earlier in the blog, I mentioned specific criteria that would affect brand loyalty and intentionally kept one point away. Surveys show that most adult customers favor particular brands by checking the online written reviews or video testimonials. If you have a happy customer base with your product or service, you could find out customers whos willing to share their experience about your products with everyone.

Influencer marketing

Influencer marketing is a way that brands involve influencers, celebrities and content creators who already have a trusted fan base to promote their products. Sending your products to a selected set of influencers who would reflect your company's standards and post their reviews as a video on social media would rapidly increase your business's brand popularity and conversion rate.

How to use your product

With technologies like quantum computing and nanotechnology, tons of medical devices would be available in the market for consumers. But using the suitable instruments made by maintaining the standard procedures would be an essential factor to consider when using these equipment in daily life.

Educate your audience

Content creation from your business should not always be about promotion. This is a fact most businesses are unaware of, and continuous promotion without any information content would negatively impact the brand image. Creating videos with a genuine intention of educating the customers in a particular field that your business is related to would positively impact brand loyalty and attract more customers. For example, suppose you have an online clothing store. In that case, you could interview the industry professionals on fashion designing and create videos to educate your customers on how to match colors, choose dress codes for different situations, and a lot more.

Thank your customers

Customers are the ultimate backbone behind your success. Create videos thanking your customers for every milestone your business achieves. This would incline the customers more towards your business and give a sense of mutuality between your business and customers.
